Avery's Animals

Welcome to Avery's Animals, this website is in honor of the memory of my beloved pets
who have passed away and to cherish those who are still with us.

I decided to develop this website after experiencing the passing of my dog Stewie,
who had been with me since my childhood. After his passing, I began to reflect on the importance of
cherishing my current pets and creating lasting memories with them. I then began to think of all
the pets that have passed away throughout my life. I then decided to develop
this website in order to honor and remember each one of them.
